DELIVERY

Put together an order of $200 or more, and we'll deliver it to your door for no extra charge! Please contact us 24 hours in advance to verify availability. We can serve whole rolls or cut them into halves so your guests can sample everything.

VENUE RENTAL

Make your next event more memorable by hosting it in one of our cozy shacks. Each space is unique and offers a flexible menu that can accommodate all palates and dietary needs. However, choosing a venue is the one decision that will have the largest impact on your event.

ON SITE

We'll also happily travel to your event. Our friendly staff can set up shop with everything necessary to serve up lobster, crab & shrimp rolls fresh off the griddle, chilled tails and jonah crab sides, seasonal tail salads, award winning soups and more.

TRUCK

The Luke's Food Trucks, Nauti Mobile and 2Nauti, are sure to make a splash at your party when they roll up curbside. Not only do Nauti and 2Nauti offer our complete menu but Nauti is also Zagat's #1 rated food truck in NYC. So don't miss out on this opportunity.
